How does it work?

  • Build Your Own Taco: Just like a salad bar, you get to choose from a wide variety of fresh ingredients. From seasoned meats and vibrant veggies to delicious sauces and crunchy toppings, the possibilities are endless.

  • Pay by Weight: Once you've crafted your perfect taco, simply weigh your creation and pay based on the weight. This way, you can enjoy as much or as little as you like, ensuring you always get great value for your meal.
